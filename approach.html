Stage Detail

How We Execute

01. Research

Objective: Understand the exact timeline of a highway incident.
Output: Field notes mapping the first 30 minutes after a truck goes down.

02. Field Validation

Objective: Verify findings on active corridors.
Output: Partnering with fleets on the Western Express Highway to test our hypotheses.

03. Understanding

Objective: Identify where the delay originates.
Output: Recognizing that the delay is a coordination gap, not a technology gap.

04. Design

Objective: Blueprint a solution tailored to operators.
Output: Wireframes and system architectures driven by field insights.

05. Technology

Objective: Build the tool.
Output: ANVIRA - an incident-to-resolution system built on verified facts.
