The Problem

Why Trucking Fleets Need ANVIRA

Highway truck incidents create immediate operational chaos — fragmented communication, missing documentation, delayed response, unnecessary downtime, and claim leakage.

Operational Confusion

Incident information scattered across calls, messages, emails, and reports. No single view of what happened or what needs to happen next.

Truck Downtime

Critical information gaps delay response coordination, recovery planning, and operational restart — costing fleet operators thousands per hour.

Missing Documentation

Critical evidence — driver statements, incident photos, timeline details — is lost or inconsistently recorded, weakening insurance claims.

Claim Leakage

Poor incident documentation and slow response coordination lead to claim rejections, reduced recoveries, and higher out-of-pocket costs.

CORE CAPABILITIES

How ANVIRA Works

Three core modules — driver-side capture, AI intelligence engine, and fleet command center — working together to eliminate incident chaos.

Driver-Side Incident Capture

Voice, photos, emergency trigger, and WhatsApp/manual reporting. Drivers capture critical evidence immediately at the scene.

AI Incident Intelligence

Automatic incident understanding, severity triage, and actionable recommendations. Structured case creation within minutes, not hours.

Fleet Command Center

Real-time incident visibility, escalation tracking, downtime monitoring, and recovery coordination from one operations dashboard.

Recovery & Claims Support

Structured documentation, automatic case records, and recovery tracking to streamline insurance workflows and reduce claim delays.

Response Flow

Incident Response Lifecycle

From incident capture to recovery coordination, ANVIRA guides fleet operators through a structured response process.

STEP 01

Incident Reported

Driver reports incident via voice, photo, emergency trigger, or WhatsApp. ANVIRA receives the alert immediately.

STEP 02

Evidence Captured

Photos, location data, driver information, and incident context are automatically collected and organized.

STEP 03

AI Triage Created

AI engine analyzes incident, determines severity, and creates structured case with recommendations for response.

STEP 04

Fleet Response Coordinated

Fleet managers receive alerts, coordinate emergency response, track recovery progress, and monitor downtime from command center.

STEP 05

Recovery & Claims

Incident documented with full audit trail. Structured records support insurance claims and operational recovery analysis.
