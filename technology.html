Core Systems

The AASIOM Technology Stack

Four interlocking systems that together create an autonomous aerial surveillance network capable of covering hundreds of kilometres continuously.

System 01

Sky-Hive

Autonomous drone docking, charging, and launch stations — the physical backbone of the surveillance network. Placed every 10-15 km along expressway corridors, each Sky-Hive manages a fleet of patrol drones.

System 02

VAYU-Net

On-board AI vision engine that processes drone camera feeds in real-time at the edge — no cloud dependency. Detects accidents, stalled vehicles, road debris, wrong-way drivers, and abnormal traffic patterns.

Computer Vision Edge Inference Night Vision Fog Penetration Anomaly Detection
  • ✓Targeting sub-second incident classification at the edge
  • ✓Designing for darkness, fog, rain, and smoke conditions
  • ✓Architecting continuous learning from operational data
System 03

MeshLink

Resilient drone-to-ground communication mesh network. Ensures unbroken data relay even in connectivity dead zones common on long highway stretches, tunnels, and Ghat sections.
