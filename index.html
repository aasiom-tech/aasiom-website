Flagship Platform

ANVIRA — AI Highway Safety & Infrastructure Intelligence

ANVIRA is AASIOM’s flagship platform for detecting, verifying, and routing highway-incident intelligence. It is being shaped for Maharashtra highway corridors, with pilot planning as the current stage.

Detects possible incidents from infrastructure data and visual inputs.
Verifies genuine events and filters noise before escalation.
Extracts severity, location, and context for faster operational decisions.
Routes verified intelligence to the right responders or agencies.
