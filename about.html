Our Origin

The Problem That
Created AASIOM

India operates some of the world's most critical and complex infrastructure — yet the intelligent systems that monitor and protect it are overwhelmingly imported. Every foreign sensor, every foreign algorithm, every foreign radio module is a dependency India cannot afford on its critical assets.

The founding team recognised that this was not just a technology gap — it was a national vulnerability. The solution was not to procure better imports. The solution was to build a company that could design and deploy sovereign systems from the ground up.

AASIOM was incorporated in 2026 in Maharashtra with a singular charter: advance India's sovereign deep-technology frontier across autonomous aerial systems, on-device edge intelligence, and indigenous semiconductor design.

Core Values

The Principles We Build By

These are not aspirations on a wall. They are the decisions we make every day about what to build, how to protect it, and who to build it for.

Sovereignty First

Every technology decision at AASIOM is evaluated through a single lens: does this reduce or increase India's dependency on foreign systems? We choose sovereignty even when imports are cheaper or faster.

Non-Negotiable

Execution Over Pitch

We are not a PowerPoint company. AASIOM measures progress by working systems and real-world deployments — not by deck revisions. Our credibility is built through what we actually build.

Operating Principle

IP-First Engineering

Every novel system architecture, algorithm, and hardware design at AASIOM is evaluated for IP protection potential before it is shared. We intend for India's intellectual property to remain in India's hands.

Strategic Posture

Mission-Scale Ambition

AASIOM is not building for a niche market segment. We are building for India's infrastructure at scale — the expressways, the airspace, the semiconductor supply chain. We think in decades, not quarters.

Long-Term Vision

Government as Partner

We design our systems with government bodies — MSRDC, NHAI, MoD — as the intended primary operators. This shapes our security thinking, our interface design, and our long-term deployment philosophy.

Deployment Model

Research at the Core

AASIOM was founded by people who believe in rigorous, first-principles problem solving. Our technology is grounded in deep analysis and research methodology, not vendor shortcuts.
