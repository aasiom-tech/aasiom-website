Fleet Health

Key Metrics for Fleet Operations

Real-time visibility into the metrics that matter most for fleet safety and operational performance.

Incident Response Time

Average time from incident report to fleet response initiation. Lower is better. Track whether response coordination is improving.

Open Incident Cases

Number of active incidents requiring monitoring, recovery action, or claims follow-up. Indicators of fleet operational load.

Documentation Completeness

Percentage of incidents with full evidence, driver statements, photos, and timeline data. Higher completeness = stronger claims.

Truck Downtime

Average hours a truck is offline after incident. Critical for understanding recovery efficiency and fleet availability impact.

Claim Leakage Risk

Percentage of claims at risk due to missing documentation, delays, or poor evidence trails. Lower is better.

Fleet Availability

Percentage of fleet trucks operational and available for cargo movement. Directly reflects incident impact on revenue.
